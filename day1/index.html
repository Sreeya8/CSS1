ABOUT

Conçu is a cake boutique that specializes in bespoke celebration cakes tailored to the taste and specifications of the client. Every product is crafted with elegance and perfection to produce a luxurious dessert that combines subtle flavours and myriad textures. The chef’s love for fine European pastry is reflected in his creations- many of these are traditional classics with modern interpretations.
